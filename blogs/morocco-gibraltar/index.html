Morocco & Gibraltar

DAY I

We arrived in Marrakesh Medina Airport at about 7:06 a.m. Since check-in at our riad was from 3:00 p.m., we decided to walk from the airport all the way to the medina on foot. The road was long, straight, and full of traffic, with not much to see along the way.

Upon arriving in the medina, we bought khobz, the traditional Arab bread, for breakfast along with traditional tea. After that, we walked around the medina until we were allowed to check in to our accommodation. Just after arriving, we fell asleep because we had not slept at Malaga Airport the previous night.

After waking up, we went to get some tagine and then went straight back to bed.

DAY II

The following day, we visited Ben Youssef Madrasa, which was at the time the largest Islamic college in the Maghreb. After that, we spent the rest of the day exploring the medina and had couscous for dinner.
